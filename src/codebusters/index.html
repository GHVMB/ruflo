Our Story

Healthcare Coding
Reimagined

Founded in 2008 by industry veteran Linda Kobayashi, Codebusters set out to deliver scalable, cost-effective coding solutions that guarantee accuracy, compliance, and smooth revenue cycle management.

We've since evolved into the industry's most flexible healthcare talent partner — helping hospitals and healthcare providers fill critical roles across clinical, administrative, health information, IT, and facilities support.

Our philosophy is simple: start with a conversation, not a resume. We identify professionals with exactly the right mix of skills, culture fit, and familiarity to handle each client's unique challenge.
